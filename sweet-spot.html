Sweet Spot

An inter-species caress in all the sweet spots. Filmed in Scotland, Newfoundland, and New York state.

Artist Bio

Jillian McDonald is a Canadian artist living in Brooklyn and Troy, NY Exhibitions include Undercurrent and FiveMyles in Brooklyn, The Art Gallery of Regina in Saskatchewan, Esker Foundation in Calgary, and AxeNéo7 in Québec. A CBC Ideas documentary profiles her videos, which were also reviewed in The New York Times and Canadian Art. Critical discussion appears in The Transatlantic Zombie by Sarah Lauro. Awards include grants from The New York Foundation for the Arts and The Canada Council for the Arts, and residencies including the Headlands Center for the Arts in California, the Glenfiddich Canadian Art Prize in Scotland, and The Arctic Circle in Svalbard.
