Dominika Jezewska

A Year of Dreaming

View project (opens in a new tab)

A year of dreaming was a year long project (2020-2021), dreamed up between reality, VR and photoshop. A single visual work was created and published every single day for 365 days. Born out of creative frustration and mounting creative blocks it was a way to find self in the overwhelming creative anxiety and to discover a way to express artistic self in the day to day regardless of circumstances.

No expectations, no plan, a pure visual experimentation to test the limits of each medium and explore them further, to discover new landscapes and worlds in the process, teleporting myself through a dream into a different dimension every single day.

Artist Bio

Dominika Jezewska is a multidisciplinary artist and designer with a background in fashion, 3D art, and virtual reality. Born in Warsaw, Poland she holds a BFA in Fashion Design from Parsons School of Design. Her art has graced exhibitions worldwide, from China to Oman, Poland to the Netherlands, and the USA, earning recognition in numerous publications. With her versatile background, Dominika seamlessly integrates diverse creative realms to craft immersive and captivating artistic experiences. Through her intricate designs, fashion-forward aesthetics, and innovative use of virtual reality, she pushes the boundaries of traditional mediums, inviting viewers to explore new dimensions of perception, challenging the way we interact with and experience art.
